CROWNÆTH
Hard Techno DJ & Producer
Based near Butzbach / Frankfurt

Genres:
Hard Techno, Industrial,
Hard Dance, Neo Rave,
Hardtech, Raw Style,
Dark Techno

Music has always been there. Long before the decks, CROWNÆTH was the one running the speakers at every party — until enough people said the same thing: "you've got the sound, start playing it out." Three years later, DJing isn't a hobby anymore. It's a part of him.

The sound moves between Hard Techno, Industrial, Hard Dance and Neo Rave — with detours into Hardtech, Raw Style and the slower, hypnotic Dark Techno that shaped him from the start. Fast, raw, hypnotic. Built for sweat, smoke and 4am energy.

Six months deep into producing his own tracks. Cut his teeth in small clubs, warehouses and underground raves across Hessen — the kind of nights that don't make the lineup posters but stay in your head for weeks.
